Recipe: Smashed Burgers

Description

Burgers are sandwich consisting of one or more patties of ground meat, placed inside a sliced bread roll or bun. What differentiates a smashed-burger and a burger, however, is that the patties are smashed during the process of cooking. These burgers are quick, simple and best of all, tasty!

Special thanks to serious eats for the smashed burger recipe.

Ingredients

  • Soft hamburger rolls, buttered and toasted
  • Desired toppings and condiments (mayo, mustard, lettuce, tomato, onions, pickles)
  • Minced beef chuck (Preferably 80% meat, 20% fat)
  • Salt
  • Freshly ground pepper
  • Sliced cheese (Good melting cheese)

Steps

  1. Prepare your mince beef into ~55g balls
  2. Prepare burger bun by laying toppings on the bottom half of bun. Have it nearby and ready
  3. Preheat your frying pan over high heat
  4. Place balls of beef in pan and smash down with a stiff metal spatula, using a potato masher and make sure the patties are slightly wider than the burger bun
  5. Season the patties generously with salt and pepper
  6. Flip after ~45 seconds or the patties are browned, immediately placing a slice of cheese over 1 patty.
  7. Remove from pan and transfer to waiting burger bun
  8. Serve with chips and your favourite drink!
